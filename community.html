Community

Whether you ran into a problem, have questions regarding Neovim, or just want to see what's new, reach out to the Neovim community on one of the following sites:

  • Neovim on Gitter (accessible via IRC)
  • Neovim on Twitter
  • Neovim Google Group
  • /r/neovim on Reddit
  • #neovim on Freenode
  • vi StackExchange (use the neovim tag)

If you want to report an issue or give feedback to the developers, please use Neovim's issue tracker on GitHub.
