FAQ

How can I sponsor Neovim?

Neovim operates a campaign to fund ongoing development. This makes it possible for core developers to work full-time for a month or longer, accelerating projects like Lua stdlib, treesitter parser engine, LSP framework, extended marks, embedded terminal, job control, RPC API, and remote UIs.

Who are the sponsors?

Current sponsors are featured in the left column of this page. These are companies or individuals contributing a monthly amount to help support Neovim.
